{{_('The Integrated Publishing Toolkit (IPT) is the standard tool used to publish biodiversity data to GBIF.')}}
{{_('IPTs are typically hosted and managed by national, thematic, or regional GBIF nodes, which provide infrastructure and support to data publishers.')}}

{{_('Some IPTs are operated by the GBIF Secretariat as shared, cloud-based services, while others are hosted locally by nodes or trusted data-hosting centres. The level of support and assistance may vary depending on the hosting institution.')}}

{{_('To find an existing IPT and identify a suitable hosting option, consult the official GBIF IPT directory:')}} https://www.gbif.org/ipt {{_('.')}} {{_('We suggest using the IPT of DATA TERRA :')}} https://ipt.data-terra.org/ {{_('.')}}
{{_('Publish your dataset on the IPT')}}
{{_('Connect to the IPT instance that will host your data. Click on Manage Resources and Create new.')}}
{{_('You need to choose a short name for your dataset and the type of data.')}}

{{_('Upload your Darwin Core Archive by selecting Import from an archived resource and loading the DwCA ZIP file.')}}
{{_('Click Create. All metadata and data should be automatically pre-filled.')}}

{{_('Change the visibility from private to public in the tab Visibility.')}}

{{_('In the Publication tab, click Edit, set the Publishing Organization to EcoTaxa, save your changes.')}}

{{_('You can now publish your data.')}}

{{_('To register the dataset with GBIF, go to the Registration tab, click Register.')}}
